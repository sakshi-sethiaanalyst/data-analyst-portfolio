# Retail Commercial Performance Dashboard
**Tools:** Power BI | DAX | Advanced Excel

## Overview
An end-to-end commercial analytics dashboard tracking revenue, 
margin, and SKU performance across regions — built to mirror 
real-world retail reporting used in FMCG and retail operations.

## Business Problem
Retail operations teams need granular visibility into margin 
performance, pricing variance, and slow-moving inventory to make 
timely commercial decisions.

## What I Built
- Star-schema data model in Power BI connecting sales, product, 
  and region tables
- DAX-driven margin analytics tracking gross margin %, revenue 
  by SKU, and pricing variance
- Excel model for data validation and pre-processing before 
  loading into Power BI

## Key Insights
- Identified bottom 20% SKUs by margin contribution
- Highlighted pricing variance trends driving margin erosion
- Built supplier cost vs. revenue comparison view for 
  commercial negotiation support
