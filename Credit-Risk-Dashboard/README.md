# Credit Risk & Collections Analytics Dashboard
**Tools:** Power BI | DAX | SQL

## Overview
A risk-focused analytics dashboard analysing delinquency trends, 
Days Past Due (DPD) segmentation, and collection efficiency metrics 
— designed for BFSI collections and operational risk use cases.

## Business Problem
Collections teams need clear visibility into delinquency patterns, 
high-risk account segments, and recovery efficiency to prioritise 
outreach and reduce credit losses.

## What I Built
- Power BI dashboard segmenting accounts by DPD buckets 
  (0–30, 31–60, 61–90, 90+ days)
- DAX measures tracking collection rate %, recovery trends, 
  and portfolio risk exposure
- SQL queries for delinquency classification and account 
  segmentation

## Key Insights
- Identified highest-risk DPD segments driving portfolio 
  delinquency
- Tracked month-on-month recovery rate trends
- Built early warning indicators for accounts approaching 
  90+ DPD threshold
